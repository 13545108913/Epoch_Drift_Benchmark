1. **Scope Creep**: Stay focused on subtask goals and avoid unnecessary detours
2. **Element Assumptions**: Never assume element indices remain stable across page updates
3. **Platform Behavior Assumptions**: Test platform functionality systematically before complex operations
4. **Context Accumulation**: Implement proactive context management to prevent token limit issues
